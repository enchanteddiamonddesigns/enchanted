Policies

FAQ & booking policies

Please read before booking so you know exactly what to expect.

Do I need a deposit to book?
Some services require a small non-refundable deposit (shown in the service name). It goes toward your total and the remaining balance is due at the appointment.
What is your cancellation policy?
You can reschedule your appointment once within your notice window (set in Acuity – e.g. 24–48 hours). Late cancellations or no-shows will forfeit the deposit.
Can I come with mascara or strip lash glue on?
Please arrive with clean, makeup-free eyes to get the best retention. Arriving with heavy eye makeup or strip glue may cut into your lashing time.
Do you accept foreign fills?
This depends on the condition of your current set. Please message me photos of your lashes before booking, or book a removal + fresh set for the best result.

Studio reminders

✧ Please arrive on time so we can use your full appointment for lashing.
✧ If you are more than 15 minutes late, your appointment may need to be rescheduled and your deposit may be forfeited.
✧ No extra guests or children in the treatment room for safety and comfort reasons.
✧ If you have any eye infections, irritation, or recent surgery, please wait until fully healed before booking.
